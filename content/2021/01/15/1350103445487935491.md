I’ve owned or used all three unibody MacBook Pro (2010, 2013, 2017) and the 2017 is my least favorite of all of them.

It has nothing to do with the keyboard (no crumbs!), or the Touch Bar, or the ports (USB-C FTW).

It’s that the fans, run, constantly, under the slightest load.
